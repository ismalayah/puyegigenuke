RevPro &amp; OTT Vet Ryan Smile Passes Away, Wrestling World Pays Tribute

Publish date: 2024-09-10

The wrestling world is in mourning today as Ryan Smile has passed away.

The native of Birmingham, England, was a mainstay on the U.K. scene and had success as a singles star. Smile held the RevPro Undisputed British Cruiserweight Champion in 2017, was the Over The Top No Limits Champion that same year for 182 days, and was also a three-times Relentless Division Champion in Kamikaze Pro.
