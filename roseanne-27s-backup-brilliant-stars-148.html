Roseanne's Backup (Brilliant Stars 148)

Publish date: 2024-07-06

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Roseanne's Backup (Japanese: ハマナのバックアップ Hamana's Backup) is a Supporter card. It was first released as part of the Brilliant Stars expansion.

Card text

Choose 1 or more:
  • Shuffle a Pokémon from your discard pile into your deck.
  • Shuffle a Pokémon Tool card from your discard pile into your deck.
  • Shuffle a Stadium card from your discard pile into your deck.
  • Shuffle an Energy card from your discard pile into your deck.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art card, and as a Secret Rainbow Rare in the Brilliant Stars expansion, first released in the Japanese Star Birth expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Nagomi Nijo, while the Full Art and Rainbow Rare prints feature artwork by kirisAki. In Japan, a Mirror Holofoil S-P Promotional version of the Regular print was awarded to winners of New Battle events held at participating Pokémon Card Gym venues between April 8 and July 14, 2022. These events required players to use at least four Pokémon cards with regulation mark 'E' and 'F' in their decks.[1] This print features the Pokémon Card Gym logo.
