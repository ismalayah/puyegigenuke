Sword &amp; Shield - Brilliant Stars TCG expansion announced; more details on new Pokmon VStar mechani

Publish date: 2024-07-20
After being teased earlier this month, The Pokémon Company International has officially announced today the next expansion in the Pokémon Trading Card Game, Sword & Shield—Brilliant Stars, which will be released worldwide on February 25, 2022. More details on the new Pokémon VSTAR mechanic were also unveiled, along with 9 new cards.

Pokémon VSTAR evolve from Pokémon V. Each Pokémon VSTAR has a VSTAR Power—an Ability or attack—with an extremely powerful effect that can decide the outcome of a battle. Players can use only one VSTAR Power per game, and those who use a VSTAR Power must flip over a VSTAR marker during the battle.

These Pokémon VSTAR have high HP and high-damage attacks, and they give up two Prize cards when Knocked Out.

Arceus, Charizard, Shaymin, and Whimsicott will appear as Pokémon VSTAR in the expansion. It will also include a special Trainer Gallery subset of 30 cards with artwork featuring fan-favorite Trainers and Pokémon.

Brilliant Stars will be made available in booster packs, the Elite Trainer Box, and special collections.

The Pokémon TCG: Sword & Shield—Brilliant Stars expansion includes the following:

  • 20 Pokémon V and 15 full-art Pokémon V
  • Four new Pokémon VSTAR
  • Three new Pokémon VMAX
  • 22 Trainer cards and six full-art Supporter cards
  • One new Special Energy card
The Leafeon VSTAR Special Collection and Glaceon VSTAR Special Collection will become available in the first quarter of 2022. Each of these VSTAR Special Collections includes a SWSH Black Star Promo card featuring Leafeon V or Glaceon V, an etched promo card and etched oversize card featuring Leafeon VSTAR or Glaceon VSTAR, an acrylic VSTAR marker and five booster packs.
